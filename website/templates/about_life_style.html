About Life Style Dataset

The Life Style dataset used in this study was sourced from Kaggle (Essa, 2025). It contains fitness participant information created for recommendation systems and fitness applications, and was later expanded synthetically from 973 to 20,000 rows while maintaining logical consistency between features.

Description of Final Dataset

equipment_needed Nominal categorical Object (String) Equipment required to perform the exercise.
